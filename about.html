About the Conference

A world-class gathering in the heart of Tunisia, uniting visionaries, scientists, and innovators to shape the future of materials science.

Welcome

We are delighted to invite you to attend the forthcoming conference event, MATERIAUX 2025.

This conference will be held during the period September 07-09, 2025 in the awesome city of Hammamet (Tunisia), with its rich history and beautiful landscapes.

The selected period promises excellent weather and favorable conditions for both professional engagement and leisure activities. This conference offers an ideal setting for a scientific discourse associated to a cultural exploration.

Venue & Service

As before, the service and venue will be “5 stars” quality (international rating) with ultra soft all-inclusive service at the “MEHARI HOTEL” situated at “YASMINE HAMMAMET”. In addition to the high scientific level of the meeting, we also like to give an opportunity to our guests for unforgettable holidays together with the best scientific event.

Conference Highlights

MATERIAUX 2025 Conference will highlight advances in all major disciplines of functional, smart materials and emergent applications.

Exhibitions will take place in the hall of the hotel of the conference and some talks will be given by the managers of these companies to present their innovative equipments.

Program

The program will feature invited presentations by internationally recognized scientists. Contributed oral and poster presentations will complete the program.

Join Us in Hammamet

We are really looking forward to welcome you at the famous city of Yasmine Hammamet (Hammamet) with your active participation and where you will certainly get a real treat in terms of science, culture and social aspects.

Don’t miss this opportunity to be accompanied by your family to this wonderful place of Tunisia.
